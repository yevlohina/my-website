Hi, I am Yevheniia Lohina :)

Why I chose design

I moved into design after years in marketing because I wanted to create not just campaigns, but experiences that really help people. Design lets me combine strategy, empathy, and aesthetics — and see real impact in what I do.

What inspires me

I’m inspired by human stories, meaningful visuals, and small details that make daily life easier or more beautiful. I love when design feels honest, emotional, and thoughtful.

My goals

I want to grow as a UX/UI designer and keep learning every day — from users, colleagues, and real projects.

My main goals are:

  • to build digital products that feel intuitive, emotional, and inclusive;
  • to work on meaningful projects that help people adapt, learn, or feel connected;
  • to refine my design process — from research to prototypes — and become stronger in both UX and visual design;
  • to join a creative team that values empathy, curiosity, and collaboration.
